Ken's All-Star Movers

"From The Team That Brought You Reci-Pro!"

Another project that was put together featuring me and more of my friends behind the Reci-Pro project.

We proceeded with this idea of a hypothetical client and set out to create a would-be website for the scenario of behing hired by a moving company.

The site serves as the perfect place to book or schedule moving appointments with the company as well as keep track of moving history with the company. It has an emphasis on user interconntectivity, for example you can communicate with other users or leave reviews or comments about your own experiences.

This project is a Full Stack MERN web application, with both front-end and back-end code, client and server sides. My duties covered the models and the seeds for both client and server side.
