Reci-Pro!

Concept. Code. Completetion.

Another excellent collaborative effort between me and friends. This time made to showcase where we were at the next level in our education together.

The concept was simply a food related website with a more practical twist on user activity with the site but more specifically among each other. Recipes can be posted, shared directly or searched by title or even down to the ingrediants used!

This project was put together using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, Mysql2, Nodemon, Express, Handlebars, Dotenv and Bcrypt. I in particular had the responsibility of creating the seeds and models.
