Who Am I?

Filmmaker turned developer, living in the desert

Hello! My name is Phillip 'Philly' Mock! I'm a Full Stack Web Developer out of Phoenix with a background in Film & Media Production and a life-long dedication to learning.

Additionally, I'm very effective at combining creativity and problem solving to develop user-friendly applications, and I'm known amongst my peers for strong wit and attention to detail no matter the complexity of the project.

I started my web development journey in U of A's online bootcamp program. Combined with Codecademy, freeCodeCamp, Udemy & self-study, it's been a fulfilling start and I look forward to experiencing and learning more as I transition this once casual hobby into a thriving lifestyle.

The next step in my journey largely includes expanding my education and pursing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. Something I plan to achieve by the end of 2025.
