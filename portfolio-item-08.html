Flick MeA Winner!

Movie Night | Pick-A-Flick

This is a personal project, one early in development at that. But I got enough to show off a good starting point.

If you've been here long enough, you'll notice I have background education in media and broadcast production. It may not suprise you to learn that I'm something of a film buff.

As such, I decided to test and flex my level of skills and knowledge and create a simple application that prompts user input in the form of movie titles. After the user is satisfied with their list of options, a simple shuffle button will pick one of the bunch and proudly display your movie of choice!!

This project is currently being made in Visual Studio Code with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and JQuery.
